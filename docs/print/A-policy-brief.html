Executive summary01

I work in disability-inclusive development, with field grounding across India and policy assignments at international level. Currently Advisor - Inclusion, Systems & Partnerships at Snehadhara Foundation, Bengaluru. Most recently completed a tenure as Assistant Professor then Consultant with the Office of Disability Inclusion at Azim Premji University (Dec 2022 - Mar 2026). Two assignments with the WHO Disability Unit in Geneva contributed to the Global Report on Health Equity for Persons with Disabilities (2022); World Bank work on the TN RIGHTS project supported the Government of Tamil Nadu; and an earlier posting in Bihar produced the State Disability Inclusion Guideline, in implementation since 2020.
